# 🩺 Medical Mastery OS v0.1.0

> [!TIP]
> **New here? Start with the [WELCOME.md](WELCOME.md) guide for a warm introduction to your new Clinical Research Assistant.**

A professional, automated system for medical professionals and students to master clinical knowledge, foundational sciences, and board exams.

**Antigravity**: Your Clinical Research Assistant. Just drop medical journals, textbooks, or clinical guidelines into the `input-library/`, and Antigravity will synthesize them into "Fact Sheets" and study sessions.

---

## 🚀 How to Start

1.  **Drop your files**: Put your Medical PDFs (First Aid, Pathoma, Journals) into `input-library/`.
2.  **Orchestrate**: Ask Antigravity to `/orchestrate medical` to process new materials.
3.  **Master**: Start a clinical learning session with `/learn <topic>`.
4.  **Review**: Use `/review` to keep your clinical knowledge fresh via spaced repetition.

**Common Commands:**

| Step | Command | Effect |
|------|---------|--------|
| 1 | `/intel` | Get your daily clinical brief (Guidelines, New Research). |
| 2 | `/orchestrate` | Process any new files in your library. |
| 3 | `/learn <slug>` | Start a guided learning session on a medical topic. |
| 4 | `/review` | High-yield review of clinical concepts. |

## Clinical Architecture

- **Foundational Sciences**: Anatomy, Physiology, Pathology, Pharmacology.

- **Progress Tracker**: Real-time status of your board preparation and clinical rotations.
