# 🏥 Clinical Infrastructure Overview

This document outlines the professional-grade infrastructure that powers your Medical Mastery OS. It is designed to be secure, efficient, and 100% focused on clinical excellence.

---

## 1. The Core Components

### 🧠 The Intelligence Layer (Antigravity)
This is the "brain" of the system. It uses advanced medical language models to:
- Parse complex clinical literature.
- Synthesize "High-Yield" summaries.
- Conduct Socratic tutoring sessions during Clinical Rounds.

### 📊 The Data Layer (Knowledge Base)
Your clinical notes and "Fact Sheets" are stored in a structured, hierarchical format:
- `foundational-sciences/`: The building blocks of medicine.
- `organ-systems/`: Integrated clinical knowledge.
- `clinical-practice/`: Guidelines and ethical standards.

### 📟 The Operations Layer (CLI Dashboard)
The `study.py` tool provides a professional interface for:
- Tracking USMLE board preparation progress.
- Monitoring clinical mastery levels.
- Running system diagnostics to ensure your data is secure.

---

## 2. Information Security & Privacy

We understand that medical information is sensitive. The system is designed with a "Privacy First" mindset:
- **Local Storage**: Your primary knowledge base resides on your machine.
- **Git Atlas**: All changes are tracked via an internal "Atlas," providing a complete audit trail of your learning progress.
- **No Marketing Tracking**: Unlike commercial medical apps, this system does not track your data for advertising purposes.

---

## 3. Integrated Tooling

The system integrates with several professional tools to enhance your learning:
- **Spaced Repetition Engine**: Powers your daily review sessions.
- **Clinical Guideline Feed**: Feeds the daily `/intel` brief with the latest society updates.
- **Foundry Extraction**: A high-precision tool for converting complex medical PDFs into clear, readable Markdown notes.

---

## 4. Maintenance & Scalability

The "System Doctor" (`./study.py doctor`) ensures that your clinical infrastructure is always healthy. It checks for:
- Missing medical study plans.
- Broken links between clinical notes.
- Configuration integrity.
