# USMLE Step 1 Mastery Plan

A high-yield roadmap for foundational science mastery.

## Phase 1: Foundational Sciences
- [ ] **Pathology**: General Principles (Inflammation, Neoplasia).
- [ ] **Pharmacology**: Autonomics, General Principles.
- [ ] **Physiology**: Cellular and Integrated systems.
- [ ] **Biochemistry**: Molecular biology, Genetics.

## Phase 2: Organ Systems
- [ ] **Cardiovascular**: Hemodynamics, EKG, Valve diseases.
- [ ] **Renal**: Acid-base, Electrolytes, Glomerular disease.
- [ ] **Neurology**: Neuroanatomy, Stroke, Neurodegenerative.
- [ ] **GI**: Motility, Liver pathology.

## Phase 3: Clinical Integration
- [ ] **Microbiology**: Bacteriology, Virology, Mycology.
- [ ] **Immunology**: Hypersensitivity, Transplant.
- [ ] **Biostatistics**: Study design, Probability.
